Core Service

General Construction

InfraUp provides complete general construction services for projects of all sizes — from structural groundwork and civil works to full finishing. We manage every stage of the build with a dedicated site team, ensuring precision and compliance with UAE building codes throughout.

Our general construction scope covers foundations, concrete structures, steel framing, masonry, MEP coordination, internal and external finishes, and project handover.

  • Civil & Structural Works
  • Concrete & Masonry
  • MEP Coordination
  • Site Management
  • Finishing & Handover
  • Compliance & Inspections
Residential

Residential Projects

We design and construct custom villas, townhouses, and apartment developments that prioritize comfort, functionality, and lasting build quality. Every residential project is approached with the same care and attention we would bring to our own homes.

Our residential team works closely with clients to understand spatial needs and lifestyle requirements, translating them into well-constructed living spaces that stand the test of time.

  • Custom Villas & Homes
  • Apartment Buildings
  • Townhouse Complexes
  • Boundary Walls & Landscaping
  • Interior Fit-Out
  • Utility & MEP Works
Commercial

Commercial Projects

InfraUp delivers commercial construction solutions for office buildings, retail outlets, warehouses, and mixed-use developments. We understand that commercial spaces must balance functionality, aesthetics, and operational efficiency — and we build accordingly.

  • Office Buildings
  • Retail & Showroom Spaces
