About InfraUp Construction

A decade of building trust, structures, and lasting relationships across the UAE.

Who We Are

InfraUp Construction Company Sole Proprietorship L.L.C. is a fully licensed construction firm based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Founded with a clear purpose — to deliver exceptional construction quality with integrity — we have grown into a trusted partner for residential, commercial, and general construction projects across the emirate.

Operating from our office in Musaffah M4, we serve clients throughout Abu Dhabi with a hands-on, detail-oriented approach. Our team of skilled engineers, site supervisors, and tradespeople ensures every project is delivered on time, within budget, and built to the highest standards.

Whether you're building your family's dream home, expanding a commercial facility, or renovating an existing space, InfraUp brings the expertise and dedication needed to make it happen — right.

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every project we undertake

Our Mission

To deliver construction projects of the highest quality — on time and within budget — while upholding the strictest standards of safety, craftsmanship, and client communication. We are committed to turning our clients' visions into lasting, real-world structures they can be proud of.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Abu Dhabi's most reliable and client-focused construction partner — a company synonymous with structural excellence, professional integrity, and sustainable building practices that contribute positively to the UAE's built environment.

Our Promise

Every project — regardless of size or scope — receives our full attention and expertise. We treat your project as our own, applying the same rigor to a small office fit-out as we do to a large-scale residential development.

Our Approach

We combine traditional construction values — precision, durability, and craftsmanship — with modern project management techniques and materials, ensuring every structure is built for the demands of today and the expectations of tomorrow.
